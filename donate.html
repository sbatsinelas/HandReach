Get Involved With HandReach


Donate

Your contribution can help make a difference in a child's life. Donations can be made to HandReach by mail or online.

HandReach is registered as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We will issue you a donation receipt for tax purposes. If you would like to donate by mail, please download a donation form here. Checks can be made payable to "HandReach" and can be sent to:

Handreach
P.O. Box 763
East Falmouth, MA 02536

If you would like your donation earmarked for a specific project or purpose, please feel free to note that in the memo line.

You may also make a secure payment online via Paypal. You will receive an acknowledgement email from HandReach once we receive your donation.

Volunteer

HandReach is an all-volunteer organization, and we can only grow and flourish with the help and ideas of individuals like you who are willing to devote time and energy to the cause of helping kids recover from trauma. Whether you wish to fundraise, participate in a medical mission, or simply volunteer your time, HandReach welcomes you!

Please feel free to inquire via email or our Contact Form.
