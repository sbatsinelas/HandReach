General Education Fund


Since 2002, HandReach has been offering $200 education microgrants to improve rural schools in developing nations and to help students to gain access to quality education. Qualified applicants must verify that they will deliver 100% of the funds to the intended target school or students. Follow-up reports can be delivered in the form of photos, letters, or expense receipts. Past HandReach applicants include students or professionals currently studying or working in the United States who have strong ties to the schools targeted to receive funding.

If you know of a school in need, please fill out an application form and send it to HandReach for review. Click the links below to download the application.
